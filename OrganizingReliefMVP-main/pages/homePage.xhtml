About Us

Puerto Rico faces hurricanes that are hard to endure and rebuild from. In 2022 alone, 14 hurricanes hit the nation, leaving many citizens injured, displaced, or killed. We want to help those directly affected by these natural disasters and better direct resilience efforts on the ground trying to help. Organizing Relief is a software made to help allocate aid and resources to these citizens. By leading the aid providers directly to those in need, we hope to support the survival and endurance of the victims of these hurricanes.

Our Services

Our application offers aid-based functionality for Puerto Rican citizens affected by hurricanes and members of resilience efforts trying to help. Citizens can register for an account quickly and use it to directly alert aid organizations of their situation. Once daily, citizens can fill out the emergency call form, with a description of their problem and a ranking of how dire it is. Members of resilience efforts can register for an account with a valid organization code. Through that account, resilience efforts members can also fill out emergency call forms, see and respond to emergency calls, view a vulnerability ranking of different municipalities, and chat with other organizations.
